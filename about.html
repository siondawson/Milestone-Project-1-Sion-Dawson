About Me

My name is Siôn, my background is in music. I am a classically trained cellist. I also play guitar and bass. I began my career working at weddings in 2013 as a musician with my string quartet. I have also played with many wedding bands on both guitar and bass, sometimes taking on lead vocals too.

My passion for film making arose from the need to make videos for my string quartet and other musical projects. Nobody wants to book a band that has no professional videos on their website!
