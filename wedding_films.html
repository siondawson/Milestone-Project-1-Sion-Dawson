What You Get

Your wedding video..

Memories captured forever in a cinematic 7-10 minute film in full 4K ultra high definition. Important stages of the day such as the ceremony and speeches will be filmed from at least 3 camera angles to capture more of the day. As an optional extra I can create a full edit of your ceremony and speeches.

Filmed and edited by me..

When you book me, I will work with your from booking to wedding day to project delivery. All of the examples shown are my work. No booking 3rd party videographers to film the day, no outsourcing to other editors. What you will receive will be 100% my work.

No time limit!

I will arrive in good time to capture your preparation and stay until I feel I have captured every moment. More footage = more material to make an amazing wedding film which you can cherish forever. No time limit means less pressure on you.

Fast turn around/ delivery times.

Whilst I am filming your wedding, I will be outlining ideas for the final edit in my mind. I only accept a small number of film projects and will get to work on your film immediately after the wedding. This allows me to deliver your film within just a couple of weeks.

An approach which suits you.

I will work with in a way in which you’re comfortable. If performing to the camera isn't your thing, no problem. I can create a beautiful wedding film completely unobtrusively leaving you more time to enjoy your day. If you’d prefer to perform a bit for the camera, there are a series of ‘shots’ we can work together to capture.

Drone footage included!

A whole different perspective - at no extra charge! I will take arial cinematic shots of your venue and surrounding area at no extra charge. Subject to weather conditions, venue approval and other local factors.
